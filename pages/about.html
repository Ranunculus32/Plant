History

Tina's Plant Collection is a product of years of careful labor. It all started when I moved to Sweden,
three years ago. The garden we had is plain and almost empty, save for some bushes and wildflowers growing
here and there. At first I started buying plants from local garden centers but filling up our 800sqm lot takes
a lot more than I can afford. And so I started searching online for cheaper source. That's where I discovered that
buying roots and bulbs from Holland or UK is a lot cheaper. Every late summer and autumn, I ordered for
next year's garden bed. And three years later, I end up with multiple plants of different varieties. The
collection is growing as our lawn is shrinking. It will be many more years of buying and planting until I
I will have my ideal garden. But for now, I am very happy with the result and is glad to share my passion to you.
